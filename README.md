# 🚗 Insurance Lead Conversion & Marketing Performance Dashboard

An end-to-end Business Analytics project focused on analyzing insurance leads, customer conversions, revenue performance, marketing effectiveness, and customer segmentation. The project follows a complete analytics workflow from **data cleaning in Python** to **interactive dashboarding in Power BI**, providing actionable business insights for decision-making.

---

## 📌 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze the insurance lead funnel and identify key factors influencing policy conversions, revenue generation, and marketing campaign performance. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs, evaluate campaign effectiveness, and understand customer behavior across different vehicle and demographic segments.
